Classic Apple Pie

A warm and comforting Thanksgiving favorite made with spiced apples baked inside a golden, flaky crust.

Preparation Time

Total: 1 hour 15 minutes
Preparation: 30 minutes
Cooking: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 6–7 cups sliced apples
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 double pie crust

Instructions

  1. Mix apples with sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice.
  2. Place mixture in pie crust and dot with butter.
  3. Add top crust, seal edges, and cut vents.
  4. Bake at 425°F for 45 minutes.
  5. Let cool before slicing.

Sweet Potato Casserole

A creamy, rich, and sweet Thanksgiving side dish topped with buttery pecans or fluffy marshmallows.

Preparation Time

Total: 55 minutes
Preparation: 20 minutes
Cooking: 35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mashed sweet potatoes
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup mini marshmallows
  • 1 cup chopped pecans mixed with brown sugar + butter

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Spread mixture in greased baking dish.
  3. Add pecans or marshmallows on top.
  4. Bake at 350°F for 30–35 minutes.
  5. Let cool slightly before serving.
