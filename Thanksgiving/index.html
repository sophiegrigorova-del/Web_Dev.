What is Thanksgiving?

Thanksgiving is a holiday centered on gratitude, community, and the celebration of the harvest. Why Celebrate?

Thanksgiving encourages gratitude and strengthens relationships. It brings families and friends together to share food, stories, and meaningful traditions. Beyond the meal, it offers a chance to reflect on growth, support others, and appreciate the moments that make life brighter.
