Dictionary

Adamant

[aa-duh-ment]

1: a stone (such as a diamond) formerly believed to be of impenetrable hardness. 2: an unbreakable or extremely hard substance

Noun

Originated from Latin America

Honor

[aa-nr]

1: To regard or treat (someone) with admiration and respect, to regard or treat with honor. 2: Adherence to what is right or to a conventional standard of conduct: 3: Something regarded as a rare opportunity and bringing pride and pleasure; a privilege

Verb or noun

Orginated from Latin America

Alliance
