Breakfast

A Sweet Tangy Granola Bowl has to be my favorite breakfast! Thick, creamy Greek yogurt forms the base, then adding a cool and tangy contrast to the crunchy clusters of golden-baked granola. Slices of ripe banana add a mellow sweetness, while a handful of juicy berries bursts with freshness and antioxidants in every bite.

Dinner

Alfredo Pasta is my favorite dinner to enjoy. It is very rich and creamy with a predominant parmesan cheese flavor and a hint of garlic. It is basically a heavy cream reduction with a little bit of fresh garlic and parmesan cheese ( a good fresh grated parmesan not that powdered stuff in a jar) to help thicken it and give it that unique flavor.

Dessert

My all-time favorite dessert would have to be a Brownie Sundae. A popular ice cream dessert featuring a thick, fudgy brownie as a base, topped with scoops of ice cream, and then garnished with classic sundae toppings such as hot fudge sauce, whipped cream, nuts, and sprinkles. It combines warm, rich brownies with cold, creamy ice cream and a variety of sweet accompaniments, making it an indulgent and classic treat.
